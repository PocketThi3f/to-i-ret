Kris Lai

Founder of To-i-retto, the idea came into fruition as a way to help fellow workers be notified on their Mobile devices whenever the bathroom was ready. As for now, the application on works to serve small-to-mid sized businesses, but hopes to extend his current version for larger businesses as well.
